Welcome to hAPPy, an app to track various parameters of your life.

If you would like to be able to share these parameters, go to the settings page, check the checkbox and press "Save".

When going to bed, you can drag the black button to the "Going to sleep?"-button on the homepage. If you don't want to drag, simply tap the black button and then you can just tap the different buttons to change pages.
Fill in all the parameters and click the "Good night!"-button.
Depending on the settings you have chosen, you will either go back to the homepage or you will go to the social media page. If you go to the social media page and you're done sharing or if you don't want to share anything this time, click the "Skip"-button.

When waking up, tap (or drag the black button to) the "Waking up?"-button. Fill in the parameters again and then click the "Good morning!"-button.

When you have collected data, you can view various results by going to the "Get the results!"-page.

Some pages have an "info"-button at the top right corner which provides information for some pages (only where necessary).

It is recommended to use this app with a smartphone, but it should also work with regular computers and tablets as long as you have a browser which supports HTML5.
